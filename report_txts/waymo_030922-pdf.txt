On March 9, 2022 at 12:22 PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a passenger vehicle on Irving Street at 25th Avenue.

The Waymo AV was stopped in traffic, eastbound on Irving Street in autonomous mode. A parked car to the right of the Waymo AV began
reversing into the street and made contact with the rear right side of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4

ADS was engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver was present (in the driver’s seating position). The Waymo AV sustained minor
damage.
