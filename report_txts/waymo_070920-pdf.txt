A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) was making a turn in manual mode from westbound Palo Alto Avenue onto northbound
Glenbrook Avenue in San Francisco when it made contact with a curb. The Waymo AV test driver was making a right turn when the right

rear tire of the Waymo AV made contact with a curb at approximately 10 MPH. The Waymo AV’s right rear tire deflated following the curb
contact. No injuries were reported.
