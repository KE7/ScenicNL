On August 11, 2023 at 7:38 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in Los Angeles, California was in a collision
involving a stationary object on North Curson Avenue at Waring Avenue.

The Waymo AV was pulling over to the right side of the road, when the test driver transitioned to manual mode and made contact with a

recycling bin on the right side of the street. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was not engaged and a test driver was
operating the Waymo AV in manual mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage.
