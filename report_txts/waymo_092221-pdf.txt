On September 22, 2021 at 4:29 AM PDT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco was in a collision
involving a passenger vehicle at Lombard Street and Scott Street. The Waymo AV was stopped at a red light facing west on Lombard Street
in manual mode. A passenger vehicle approaching the rear of the Waymo AV at approximately 26 miles per hour collided with the right

rear bumper of the vehicle. Both vehicles sustained major damage. Occupants of the Waymo vehicle reported soreness in their shoulders
after leaving the scene.
