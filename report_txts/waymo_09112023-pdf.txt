On September 11, 2023 at 8:34 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving an SUV on Sansome Street near Vallejo Street.

While the Waymo AV was pulled over in the right lane on northbound Sansome Street, an SUV that was passing the parked Waymo AV
made contact with the rear driver’s side of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was not engaged and
a test driver was operating the Waymo AV in manual mode. Both vehicles sustained damage.
