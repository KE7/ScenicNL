On 01/14/2020 at 11:13 AM, the Aurora vehicle -- while at a complete stop at a red light at the intersection of Lafayette St and Benton St.
in Santa Clara -- was rear-ended on the driver's side rear fender by a Jeep Wrangler, when it attempted to navigate around the stopped
Aurora vehicle. There were no injuries to the occupants of either vehicle, nor damage to either vehicle.
