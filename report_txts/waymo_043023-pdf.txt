On April 30, 2023 at 1:53 PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision
involving an SUV on Oak Street at Octavia Boulevard.

While the Waymo AV was parked at the right side curb of the road on Oak Street, an SUV that was passing the parked Waymo AV in the
rightmost lane made contact with the rear driver’s side of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was
engaged in autonomous mode. The Waymo AV did not sustain damage, while the SUV sustained damage.
