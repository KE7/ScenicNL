On October 8, 2021 at 6:39 PM PDT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was ina
collision involving a passenger vehicle at Mission Street at Avalon Avenue. While traveling northwest on Mission Street in autonomous
mode, the Waymo AV navigated around a double-parked vehicle stopped in the right lane. After re-entering the right lane, the Waymo AV
came to a complete stop behind a second passenger vehicle, at which time the driver of the Waymo AV transitioned into manual mode. The

second passenger vehicle reversed into the Waymo AV, making contact with its front bumper. Both vehicles sustained minor bumper
damage.
