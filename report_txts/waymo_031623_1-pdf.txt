On March 16, 2023 at 2:19 AM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Bruno, CA was in a collision
involving road debris on Interstate 280 at the interchange ramp for Interstate 380 (Exit 43A).

The Waymo AV was traveling south on Interstate 280 in the rightmost exit lane for Exit 43A towards Interstate 380 when it made contact

with road debris (tire scrap). At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was (engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver
was present (in the driver’s seating position). The Waymo AV sustained damage.
