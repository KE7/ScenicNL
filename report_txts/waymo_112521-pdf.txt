On November 25, 2021 at 1:31 PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, CA made contact with
a curb at 32nd Avenue and Taraval Street.

While in manual mode, the Waymo AV test driver was completing a right turn from northbound 32nd Avenue onto eastbound Taraval
Street when the Waymo AV’s passenger side rear tire made contact with the curb at the corner of the intersection. At the time of the impact,
the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was not engaged and a test driver was operating the Waymo AV in manual mode. The Waymo AV’s
passenger side rear tire and wheel sustained minor damage.
