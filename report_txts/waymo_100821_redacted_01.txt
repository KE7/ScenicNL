On October 8, 2021 at 8:39 AM PDT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a passenger vehicle at Ellsworth Street and Jarboe Avenue. While operating in manual mode, the driver of the Waymo

AV pulled into the right parking lane to park. In the process, the Waymo AV made contact with the bumper of a parked vehicle. Both
vehicles sustained minor bumper damage.
