A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) in autonomous mode was yielding to check for cross traffic while in the slip lane to turn
from northbound Whisman Station Drive to southbound S. Whisman Road in Mountain View when it was rear-ended by a passenger

vehicle at approximately 7 MPH. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage to its rear bumper, and the passenger vehicle had minor damage
to its front bumper. There were no injuries reported at the scene.
