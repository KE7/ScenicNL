On December 19, 2019 at approximately 9:44AM, a Zoox vehicle operating in manual mode was struck on the passenger side fender by a
bus. The Zoox vehicle was traveling north on Kearny in the right most lane when the bus attempted to lane change in front of the Zoox

vehicle, impacting its front passenger side sensors and fender. The bus was traveling less than 2mph when it hit the Zoox vehicle, which
was stopped. There were no injuries.
