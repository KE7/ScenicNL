THE DRIVER WAS OPERATING THIS VEHICLE ON US HWY 710N IN L2 MODE APPROACHING THE INTERCHANGE WITH
THE US HWY 91. AS DRIVER APPROACHED AN OVERPASS, A 4-FOOT LONG STEEL ROD FELL FROM THE BRIDGE AND
BOUNCED OFF THE HIGHWAY BEFORE HITTING THE FRONT BUMPER OF THE VEHICLE. THE DRIVER TOOK MANUAL
CONTROL AND BROUGHT THE VEHICLE TO A STOP ON THE SHOULDER. DAMAGE WAS INCURRED TO THE FRONT
BUMPER INCLUDING DESTRUCTION OF A CAMERA SENSOR, LIDAR, AND RADAR. NO INJURIES WERE REPORTED BY
THE DRIVER AND HE WAS ABLE TO DRIVE FROM THE SCENE OF THE INCIDENT.
