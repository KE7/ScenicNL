A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) was stopped in autonomous mode on northbound Alma Street at Alma Village Circle in
Palo Alto when it was rear-ended. The Waymo AV was stopped in traffic for a red light when a passenger vehicle made contact with the
Waymo AV’s rear bumper at approximately 2 MPH. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage to its rear bumper and hatch, and the
passenger vehicle sustained minor damage to its front bumper. No injuries were reported.
