A Zoox vehicle in manual mode was slowing with the right of way to begin its turn to enter the 580 West Freeway on-ramp from West
Grant Line Road in Altamont, CA. Vehicle 2 exiting the 580 West Freeway off-ramp jumped the median and made contact with the front
end of the Zoox vehicle, continuing off an embankment and ending in a rollover. The other driver refused to provide any information and
was observed being treated by first responders. The California Highway Patrol provided an NCI number (9390) at the scene. Autonomy was
not engaged at any point during the drive cycle proceeding the event.
