On March 20, 2023 at approximately 8:04 PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California
was in a collision involving a pothole on Bayshore Boulevard near Jerrold Avenue.

The Waymo AV was traveling in the second lane on a four-lane section of northbound Bayshore Boulevard when the passenger side rear

tire made contact with a pothole. The tire began to deflate and the Waymo AV pulled over on a side street. At the time of impact, the
Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode.
