A Cruise autonomous vehicle (“Cruise AV”) being driven in conventional mode was involved in a collision while traveling on eastbound
Jackson Street between Stockton Street and Grant Avenue. While pulling out of a parking spot on Jackson, the driver of the Cruise AV
made contact with another vehicle parked in front of it, damaging the Cruise AV’s front bumper assembly, left headlamp assembly, and left
front radar. There were no injuries and police were not called.
