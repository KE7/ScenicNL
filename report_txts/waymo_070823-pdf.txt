On July 8, 2023 at 3:08 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (““Waymo AV”) operating in Los Angeles, California was in a collision
involving a cyclist on S. Alameda Street at 6th Street.

The Waymo AV was traveling southbound on S. Alameda Street and preparing to make a right turn onto westbound 6th Street. As the
Waymo AV was yielding to cross-traffic and preparing to make a right turn, a cyclist was traveling eastbound on 6th Street and heading the
wrong way in the bike lane. The cyclist then turned left, northbound, onto S. Alameda Street, continuing to travel the wrong direction in the
bike lane. As the Waymo AV slowly proceeded forward, the cyclist passed the Waymo AV and made contact with the rear passenger side
sensor of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver
was present (in the driver’s seating position). The Waymo AV sustained damage and the cyclist did not stop, leaving the scene without
