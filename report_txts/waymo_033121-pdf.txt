A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) traveling northbound in manual mode on 9th Street approaching Howard Street in San
Francisco was involved in a collision. The Waymo AV test driver was slowing for stopped traffic ahead when a passenger vehicle behind
the Waymo AV made contact with the Waymo AV’s rear bumper at approximately 7 MPH. The Waymo AV was traveling at approximately
2 MPH. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage to its rear bumper, and the passenger vehicle sustained minor damage to its front bumper.
The Waymo AV test driver and co-driver both sought medical attention at the scene and were transported to local hospitals.
