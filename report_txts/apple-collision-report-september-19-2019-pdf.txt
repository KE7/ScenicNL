On September 19th, 2019 at 7:58am an Apple test vehicle, which was conducting a manually driven test mission, was struck while stopped
in the left turn lane preparing to exit the Sunnyvale Community Center Park onto Manet Drive. The Apple test vehicle was stopped when
the Toyota Corolla, traveling at approximately 6 mph, swung wide outside of its lane while making a right turn into the Sunnyvale
Community Center Park entrance from Manet Drive and struck the Apple test vehicle. Both vehicles sustained minor damage to the driver's
side front bumpers. The drivers exchanged information. No injuries were reported. The police were not called.
