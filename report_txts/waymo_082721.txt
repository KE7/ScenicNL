A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision involving a passenger vehicle at
Presidio Avenue and Bush Street. The Waymo AV was stopped at a red light in autonomous mode when the passenger vehicle behind the

AV made contact with the rear of the Waymo vehicle. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage to the rear bumper. The passenger vehicle
appeared to sustain minor scuffing or no damage.
