A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) traveling northbound on Laguna Street at Vallejo Street in San Francisco was involved in a
collision. While in manual mode, the Waymo AV test driver stopped at the four-way stop sign intersection on Laguna Street at Vallejo
Street when a passenger vehicle behind the Waymo AV made contact with the Waymo AV’s rear bumper at approximately 2 MPH. The
Waymo AV was traveling 0 MPH. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage to its rear bumper, and the passenger vehicle left the scene
before exchanging information. No injuries were reported at the scene.
