On October 18, 2021 at 12:57 PM PDT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, CA was in a collision
involving an SUV at Cesar Chavez and Evans Avenue.

As the Waymo AV approached a right turn from Cesar Chavez onto Evans Avenue, it braked behind stopped traffic. An SUV following
behind the Waymo AV made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was
engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver was present in the driver’s seating position. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage to its
rear bumper and trunk, and the SUV sustained minor damage to its front bumper and hood.
