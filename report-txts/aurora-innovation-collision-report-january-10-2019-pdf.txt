On 01/10/19 at 2:52pm, an Aurora vehicle, while stopped at a red traffic light in manual mode on Townsend and 3rd, was struck in the rear
bumber by a Honda CR-V approaching from behind. There was moderate damage to the Aurora vehicle's right sensor, mount, and side
quarter panel. There was minimal damage to the Honda's front right bumper. Both vehicles were operational and pulled over on a side street
to exchange vehicle and insurance information. No injuries were reported and no police were requested nor present at the scene.
