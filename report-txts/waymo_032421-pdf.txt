A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) traveling northbound on Lomita Avenue approaching Aloha Avenue in San Francisco was
involved in a collision. While being driven in manual mode and approaching an oncoming passenger vehicle, the Waymo AV’s passenger
side view mirror made contact with an unoccupied parked passenger vehicle’s driver side view mirror at approximately 17 MPH. The

Waymo AV sustained minor damage to its passenger side view mirror, and the unoccupied passenger vehicle sustained minor damage to its
driver side view mirror. No injuries were reported at the scene.
