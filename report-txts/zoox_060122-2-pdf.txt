A Zoox vehicle in autonomous mode was stopped at a red traffic signal at the intersection of Harrison and 11th Streets in San Francisco
with its left turn signal engaged. After the traffic signal changed to green and while waiting for an opening to turn left onto Harrison Street,
the Zoox vehicle was struck by a passenger vehicle from behind. The driver of that passenger vehicle stated that he was using his phone and
not paying attention. No police or emergency services were called to the scene. The Zoox driver, front passenger, and rear passenger
reported minor injuries for which they later sought medical attention. The Zoox vehicle incurred minor damage to its rear sensors.
