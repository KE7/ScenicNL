A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision involving a passenger vehicle at
Bush Street and Grant Avenue. The Waymo AV was stopped in traffic while in autonomous mode when the passenger vehicle approached

from behind while changing lanes. The passenger vehicle struck the rear of the stopped Waymo AV traveling at 5.5 miles per hour and left
the scene. The left rear bumper of the Waymo AV sustained minor damage.
