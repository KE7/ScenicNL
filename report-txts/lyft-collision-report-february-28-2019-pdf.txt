At approximately 2:06 pm on February 28, 2019, a Lyft Autonomous Vehicle ("Lyft AV"), operating in manual mode, was struck at very
low speed from behind by a 3rd party passenger vehicle while waiting to make a protected left turn in Mountain View, CA. The driver of
the Lyft AV had been inched forward after being stopped at a red light for approximately 105 seconds on NB Shoreline Boulevard while
waiting to make a left turn onto Wright Avenue. The driver of the 3rd party passenger vehicle verbally stated that she had pressed both the
accelerator and the brake pedal simultaneously. The Lyft AV sustained minor damage to the rear bumper. The 3rd party passenger vehicle
sustained minor damage to the front bumper and grill. No injuries were reported and the police were not notified.
