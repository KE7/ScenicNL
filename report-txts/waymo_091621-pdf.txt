On September 16, 2021 at 5:14 PM PDT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a passenger vehicle at Palou Avenue and Selby Avenue. While making a left turn from Palou Avenue onto Selby
Avenue in manual mode, the Waymo AV was struck by a passenger vehicle attempting to pass the Waymo AV on the left side using the

oncoming traffic lane. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage to the left front fender. The other vehicle left the scene without exchanging
information.
