On August 10, 2022 at 5:05 PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving an SUV on Clayton Street at Market Street.

While approaching a merge onto southbound Market Street from southbound Clayton Street, the Waymo AV came to a stop to yield for
approaching traffic on the left. While the Waymo AV was stopped, an SUV approached from behind and made contact with the rear bumper
of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged. Both vehicles sustained minor damage.
