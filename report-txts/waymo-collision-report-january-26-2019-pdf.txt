A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) in manual mode was yielding to check for cross traffic while in the slip lane to turn from
southbound Shoreline Boulevard to westbound El Camino Real in Mountain View when it was rear-ended by a passenger vehicle. The

Waymo AV sustained minor damage to the rear left of the vehicle, and the passenger vehicle had minor damage to the front right of the
vehicle. No injuries were reported.
