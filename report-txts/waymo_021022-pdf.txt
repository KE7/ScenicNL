On February 10, 2022 at 8:00 PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a pole in a parking structure on Broadway at Montgomery Street.

While reversing out of a parked position in manual mode, the test driver of the Waymo AV struck a pole. At the time of the impact, the

Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was not engaged and a test driver was operating the Waymo AV in manual mode. The Waymo AV sustained
minor damage to the rear bumper.
