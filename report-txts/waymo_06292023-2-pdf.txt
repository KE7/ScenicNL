On June 29, 2023 at 11:18 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in Los Angeles, California was in a collision
involving a pick-up truck on 4th Street at S. June Street.

The Waymo AV was traveling eastbound on 4th Street and stopped at a stop sign at the intersection of S. June Street when a pick-up truck
approached the Waymo AV from behind and made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV. The Waymo AV test driver took manual
control of the Waymo AV to pull over, and the pick-up truck made further contact the Waymo AV. At the time of the initial impact, the

Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver was present (in the driver’s seating position). The Waymo
AV sustained damage.
