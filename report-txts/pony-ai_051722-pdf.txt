On May 17, 2022, a passenger vehicle reversed out of a parking spot into a Pony.ai autonomous testing vehicle’s rear bumper. At the time
of the collision, the Pony.ai vehicle was operating in autonomous mode with a safety operator in the driver’s seat and was moving at a low
speed. The Pony.ai vehicle did not sustain any damage. There were no injuries or property damage, the police were not called to the scene,
and a Pony.ai employee drove the Pony.ai vehicle back to Pony.ai’s Irvine facility.
