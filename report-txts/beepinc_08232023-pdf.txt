At approximately 5:54 p.m. PST on Wednesday, August 23, 2023, at the intersection of Seven Seas Ave and 9th Street on Treasure Island in
the city of San Francisco, a Beep-operated autonomous shuttle was proceeding in autonomous mode straight E/B after stopping at a 3-way
stop intersection when a vehicle making a left turn heading N/B proceeded out of turn resulting in a collision within the intersection. There

were no passengers on board and no injuries reported. Both vehicle sustained minor damage and police did not respond to the scene. There
were no other witness statements received.
