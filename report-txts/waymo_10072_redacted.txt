On October 4, 2021 at 11:12 PM PDT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a passenger vehicle at Mission Street and Highland Street. While operating in manual mode, a Waymo AV was waiting
at a red light facing southbound on Mission Street when a passenger vehicle approached from behind and made contact with the right rear
sensor housing of the Waymo AV. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage to the sensor housing.The passenger vehicle left the scene.
