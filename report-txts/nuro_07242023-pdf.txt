On Monday, July 24th, a Nuro Prius operating in manual mode was stopped at a red light at the intersection of Monecito and Shoreline. The
light turned green and the Nuro Prius was waiting for traffic ahead to proceed, when a vehicle approaching the intersection failed to slow

down and rear-ended the Nuro Prius. No injuries were reported. The drivers exchanged insurance information. The Nuro Prius had minor
damage to its rear bumper and lidar.
