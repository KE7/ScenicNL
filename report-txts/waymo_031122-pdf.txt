On March 11, 2022 at 10:15 AM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (““Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a parked vehicle on Vicente Street at West Portal Avenue.

While the test driver of the Waymo AV was parallel parking in manual mode on Vicente Street, the rear of the Waymo AV made contact
with the front of a parked pickup truck. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was not engaged and a test driver was
operating the Waymo AV in manual mode. The Waymo AV sustained minor damage.
