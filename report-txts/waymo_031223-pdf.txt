On March 12, 2023 at 9:28 PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a passenger car on Cesar Chavez Street at Mission Street.

The Waymo AV was stopped in the dedicated left turn lane facing west on Cesar Chavez Street, awaiting a green signal to turn onto
Mission Street. The signal for traffic proceeding west on Cesar Chavez Street turned green, and traffic in adjacent lanes began moving. The
left turn arrow signal for the dedicated turn lane occupied by the Waymo AV remained red. The passenger car that had been stopped
directly behind the Waymo AV began moving forward. The front of the passenger car made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV.
