At 1:45pm, a WeRide vehicle (Car 1) was parked at a Van Ness St. parking meter - between Eddy st. and Turk St. when another vehicle
(Car 2) began reversing in front of the vehicle in question. The reversing vehicle (Car 2) ultimately collided with the front left corner of the
WeRide vehicle (Car 1), causing minor damage to the front bumper. The reversing vehicle (Car 2) did not have any damage. Both drivers

were able to confer with one another seconds after the incident took place. The reversing vehicle's driver did not possess any form of
insurance or registration.
