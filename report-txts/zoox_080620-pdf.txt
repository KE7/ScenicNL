A Zoox vehicle was in manual mode traveling straight on Polk Street approaching the Green/Polk Street 4-way intersection at low speed. A
passenger vehicle was traveling straight on Green Street also approaching the Green/Polk intersection. Both vehicles came to their
respective stops at the intersection, then both vehicles resumed movement through the intersection at the same time and came in contact

with one another at low speed. The front right side of the Zoox vehicle made contact with the front left bumper of the passenger vehicle.
There were no injuries and the police were not called.
