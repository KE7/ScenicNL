On September 20th, 2023 at 9:00 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a passenger car on US-101 exit ramp 429B at the intersection with Bayshore Boulevard.

The Waymo AV was proceeding south on exit ramp 429B in the leftmost lane and entered the slip lane to proceed north on Bayshore
Boulevard. As the Waymo AV came to a stop for a red light at the intersection with Bayshore Boulevard, the front of the passenger car
which was following behind the Waymo AV made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV. The Waymo AV sustained damage. At the time
of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver was present.
