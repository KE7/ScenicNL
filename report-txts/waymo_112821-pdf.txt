On November 28, 2021 at 6:08 PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, CA was in a collision
involving a passenger vehicle at Clay Street at Franklin Street.

The Waymo AV was stopped at a red light on eastbound Clay Street at Franklin Street when a passenger vehicle struck the Waymo AV’s
rear bumper. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode, and a test driver was present (in

the driver’s seating position). The Waymo AV’s rear bumper sustained damage, and the passenger vehicle’s front bumper sustained
damage.
