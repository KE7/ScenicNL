On February 24, 2023 at 12:15 AM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a
collision involving a passenger vehicle on Toland Street at Hudson Avenue.

The Waymo AV was traveling southwest on Toland Street in autonomous mode. While the Waymo AV was making a left turn onto Hudson
Avenue, a passenger vehicle proceeded into the intersection from the left and made contact with the left side of the Waymo AV. The other

vehicle immediately left the scene. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. Both
vehicles sustained damage.
