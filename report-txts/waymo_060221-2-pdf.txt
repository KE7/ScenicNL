A Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) traveling northbound on Buchanan Street toward Green Street in San Francisco was
involved in a collision. While in manual mode and parked parallel to the curb on Buchanan Street, the Waymo AV test driver accelerated
from the stationary position and made contact with an unoccupied parked passenger vehicle in front of it. The Waymo AV sustained

moderate damage to its front bumper and grille, and the unoccupied parked passenger vehicle’s sustained moderate damage to rear bumper
and trunk. No injuries were reported at the scene.
