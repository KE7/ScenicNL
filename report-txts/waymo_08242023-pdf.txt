On August 24, 2023 at 4:39 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in Santa Monica, California was in a
collision involving a passenger car on Michigan Avenue at Euclid Street.

The Waymo AV was traversing a roundabout at the intersection of Michigan Avenue at Euclid Street when it slowed to a stop for other

vehicles approaching and in the roundabout. The front of the passenger car that was in the roundabout behind the Waymo AV made contact
with the rear of the Waymo AV. Both vehicles sustained damage.
