On December 2nd, 2020 at approximately 3:00pm, a Lyft Autonomous Vehicle (Lyft AV A102) operating in manual mode was stationary at
a stoplight when it was grazed by a moving third party vehicle. The incident occured on the Embarcadero near the Ferry Building heading
nothbound in San Francisco. The third party vehicle aggresively came around from behind of Lyft AV A102 and attempted to cut in front of
AV A102. The third party made contact with the Lyft AV leaving visible minor damage to the exterior casing of the Lyft AV's front left lidar
unit. The third party sustained no visible damage. The third party called 911, but law enforcement declined reporting to the scene since there

were no injuries reported.
