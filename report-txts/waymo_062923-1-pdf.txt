On June 29, 2023 at 7:50 AM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, CA was in a collision
involving a pickup truck on Jersey Street at Church Street.

The Waymo AV was traveling eastbound on Jersey Street and stopped at a stop sign at the intersection of Church Street. While yielding to
pedestrians crossing the street, a pickup truck traveling directly behind the Waymo AV made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV. At
the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was engaged in autonomous mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage.
