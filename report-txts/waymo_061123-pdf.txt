On June 11, 2023 at 3:44 PM PT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision
involving a passenger car on Santiago Street near 42nd Avenue.

The Waymo AV was parallel parked along the curb on eastbound Santiago Street when a passenger car pulled up behind the Waymo AV.
The front of the passenger car made contact with the rear of the Waymo AV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS was
engaged in autonomous mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage.
