The driver was manually driving the Mercedes-Benz vehicle on the interchange from the 105W to 405S when the vehicle in front of him
came to an abrupt stop due to slowed traffic on the freeway. The driver manually brought the Mercedes-Benz vehicle to a stop in his lane.
The vehicle behind him did not come to a stop in time and rear ended the Mercedes-Benz. There was no injury or property damage. The

Mercedes-Benz was able to drive away from the accident. Damange was sustained to the rear bumper of the Mercedes Benz and the front
bumper of the following vehicle.

At no point during the incident was the vehicle operating in autonomous mode.
