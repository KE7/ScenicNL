On May 7, 2023 at 3:27 PM PDT a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, California was in a collision
involving a cyclist on Bay Street near Mason Street.

The Waymo AV was traveling westbound in the left lane on Bay Street and had just passed Mason Street when it detected an oncoming
pickup truck traveling eastbound in the Waymo AV’s lane. The Waymo AV stopped and the pickup truck re-entered the correct lane for
eastbound traffic and passed the Waymo AV. The pickup truck was immediately followed by a large group of cyclists traveling eastbound
in multiple lanes of the roadway, including the right lanes and on the wrong side of the double-yellow centerline. The Waymo AV remained
stopped as the oncoming cyclists rode around both sides of the Waymo AV. One cyclist then made contact with the front of the stationary
