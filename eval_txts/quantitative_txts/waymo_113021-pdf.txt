On November 30, 2021 at 12:21pm PM PST a Waymo Autonomous Vehicle (“Waymo AV”) operating in San Francisco, CA was in a
collision involving an SUV on the 10 block of Castro Street.

As the Waymo AV test driver drove the Waymo AV in manual mode on southbound Castro Street between Waller Street and Lloyd Street,
the passenger side mirror of the Waymo AV made contact with a parked SUV. At the time of the impact, the Waymo AV’s Level 4 ADS

was not engaged and a test driver was operating the Waymo AV in manual mode. The Waymo AV sustained damage to its passenger side
rearview mirror.
